Privacy Policy

At WEBHOSTING, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle the limited information we collect from you.

Information We Collect

We only collect information you voluntarily provide when using our AI Assistant. This includes your search queries, which are used solely to generate a relevant response and improve the functionality of the tool. We do not collect any personal identifying information such as your name, email address, or location.

How We Use Your Information

The data from your queries is used to analyze search trends and enhance the accuracy and helpfulness of our AI Assistant. This aggregated, anonymized data helps us understand what topics are most important to our users and allows us to better serve our community.

Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your information. We do not store any of your queries on our servers long-term, and all data is processed anonymously.
